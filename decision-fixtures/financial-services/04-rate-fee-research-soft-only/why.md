# Why this fixture exists

Anonymous financial-services visitors comparing **rates and fees** need **transparent comparison and trust**, not claims about approval, creditworthiness, financial distress, or time pressure.

Engagement is kept **below** the card-shopping primary threshold so the **`finance_trust_compare_inline`** recipe wins: explainer-first copy on **`card_comparison_module`** with **`rate_and_fee_explainer`**, not a hard application resume or predatory urgency patterns.

This fixture is a **restraint-first** lock aligned with regulated-vertical policy: support research, defer application pushes, and forbid unsafe finance language on the primary decision blob.
