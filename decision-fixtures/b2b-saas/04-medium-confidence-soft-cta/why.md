# Medium confidence + soft CTA — inline only

## Commercial intent

Model and activation confidence sit in a **medium** band (~0.68 opportunity read, ~0.68 matched decision confidence after engagement blend). **Readiness is ~49** with **`soft_cta_ready`**: the visitor has earned **useful, low-friction guidance** (checklist / inline assist), not a hard commercial interruption.

## Why a primary inline decision is OK

- Deep content engagement and **implementation**-shaped concepts justify **after_scroll** inline treatment.
- **No hard CTA clicks** and **no pricing-path signal** — escalation to demo modal or lead form would overshoot trust.

## Why hard demo / lead / exit would be wrong

That would violate **“confidence earns interruption”**: medium confidence + moderate readiness must **not** unlock demo modals, lead-form walls, exit popups, or urgency framing. Those surfaces belong to **higher** confidence and **conversion-ready** posture.

## What suppression protects

Hard escalation surfaces (e.g. demo soft modal) must stay **non-show** while the inline checklist path remains the honest primary for this doctrine.
