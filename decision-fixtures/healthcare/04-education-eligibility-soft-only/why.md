# Why this fixture exists

Anonymous healthcare sessions on education or eligibility-adjacent pages should **reduce uncertainty** and support **plain-language education**, not amplify anxiety or mimic clinical judgment.

Readiness here sits **below** the eligibility-guidance recipe threshold so the runtime should prefer **`healthcare_education_next_step`** (inline next-step guide) rather than a heavier eligibility module or any provider-booking pressure.

The case locks **restraint-first** behavior: no urgency, fear, diagnosis-style, or “schedule now” copy on the primary; aggressive provider CTAs must not surface as `show` in the slot map.
